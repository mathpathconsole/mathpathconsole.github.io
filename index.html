About Us

Mathpath is about mathematics-physics. It has dynamic refreshing moment by moment. Written with Python. And it uses for mathematical subprocess is SymPy library. But not all is bases of SymPy. Created for students Pieces of MathPath. If you found a bug or mathematical error(pretty rare or no have) is probably not cause of SymPy library. This can be editing error. Please contact us swiftly, if a especially mathematical error. Also MathPath have free-text universal mathematical code(e.g. Wolfram alpha, Maple...). And there are prepared structures to do quickly solutions.

I'm Reşat Berk, studying of physics at college. And I'm thinking programming is a huge door of creativity. One day I decided that improving mathematical app with Python for mobile and desktop. First time, I'm thought for students and still developing it by free time. But I realized that: "we should improve a big useful app together willing students. It is free or cheap. Then I created a group with my college friends. Some still continue to contribute or provide feedback. The all pieces of imagine can improving.



Our mission is: Developing easy-tools(useful) for whole students or education and with students do this. Mathpath is a just starter that way. | Stanford University; "improving lives through learning." And "We envision a world where all learners are prepared to thrive in a dynamic future."
