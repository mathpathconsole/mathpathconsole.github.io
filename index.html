About Us

Mathpath is a Math Console

It has dynamic refreshing moment by moment. Written with Python. And it uses for mathematical subprocess is SymPy library. But not all is bases of SymPy. Also MathPath have free-text universal mathematical code(e.g. Wolfram alpha, Maple...). And there are prepared structures to do quickly solutions. Mathpath continue to developing if there is enough support. (Mathpath is full open-source on Github)

I'm Reşat Berk, studying of physics at university. And I'm thinking programming is a huge door of creativity. One day I decided that improving mathematical app with Python for mobile and desktop. I started to coding Mathpath, then creating a little group to contribute and test and get inspire in college...The all pieces of imagine can improving.



Our mission is: to develop easy (useful) tools (applications, etc.) for all students or the education field and to do this together with the students. In this way, Mathpath is just the beginning. | Stanford University; "improving lives through learning."

Is this a Free or Paid service?
This is completely free.
How can I support And is it necessary for me to support?
It is not mandatory for you to support, but if you choose to do so, simply contacting us is sufficient. Because of your support (both financially and morally), we are able to improve and update the application.
Will there be new applications and an advanced version of this application in the future?
Yes, Stars of the Sky is a student developer community founded with the aim of creating new tools in the scientific field, as we mentioned earlier. As we receive support and find time, new projects will be coming in the future.

Yes, our application is available on the Play Store.
